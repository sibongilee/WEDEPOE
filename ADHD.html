ADHD is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects both children and adults. It is characterized by symptoms such as inattentionG, hyperactivity, and impulsivity. These symptoms can interfere with daily functioning and development.

Common symptoms include difficulty focusing, excessive talking, and trouble staying organized. Treatment options typically include behavioral therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes.
