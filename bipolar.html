Bipolar Disorder

Bipolar disorder is characterized by extreme mood swings that include emotional highs (mania or hypomania) and lows (depression). During manic episodes, individuals may feel euphoric, full of energy, or unusually irritable. Depressive episodes bring on symptoms similar to those of depression. Treatment typically involves medication and psychotherapy to manage symptoms and improve quality of life.
